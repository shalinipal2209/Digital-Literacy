Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist for College Students:

I. Do’s

Think before posting—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate

2. Use privacy settings on platforms like Instagram and Facebook

3. Share accurate and verified information only

4. Maintain a professional online image (especially for future job opportunities)

5. Report suspicious or abusive content using in-app reporting tools

II. Dont's

Don’t share personal details like phone number, address, or college ID publicly

2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling

3. Don’t click on suspicious links or ads

4. Don’t post offensive, illegal, or inappropriate content

5. Don’t accept friend requests or messages from unknown people without verification
